An original piece of art is one that the artist has physicaly worked on. While this will more than likely be photographed or scanned for reproduction, there will only be one original. The value of this will be much higher than a print as it is the artists own work.

A print can either be a printed/photgraphed version of an original work, or it will simply be work that was made with the purpose of print - like digital art or photography.
